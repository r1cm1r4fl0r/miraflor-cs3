The Wonderful World of Stardew Valley

Stardew Valley is a video game created by the developer ConcernedApe. It is mainly a farming simulator type game with some other elements such as mining, fighting monsters, romance, and more.

Things I Like About Stardew Valley

  1. Very chill and relaxing game.
  2. Many things to do that aren't just farming related. (mining, fighting, etc.)
  3. Many different characters to talk to; all with different personalities.
  4. Nice graphics
  5. Maru
